About Us

Founded in 2021 by Shiv Sai, The Shiv's Toys name made its debut in 2022. Operates over 25 stores in 10 different cities all over India.

We are known for our high quality plastics that is perfectly safe for babies and adults alike!

Every Shiv's Toys store offers children a magical experience, and adults the rare chance to be children again. Its where they get to play with toys, watch live demos of toys, and interact with their favourite toy characters.
